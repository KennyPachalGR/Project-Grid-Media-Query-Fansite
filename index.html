Synopsis

In a world where ash falls from the sky and mist dominates the night, a group of rebels known as the "Skaa" plot to overthrow the oppressive Lord Ruler. Vin, a young street urchin with hidden powers, is recruited by Kelsier, a charismatic leader of the rebellion. As they navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue and magical abilities, Vin discovers her own strength and the true nature of the Final Empire.

About the Author

Brandon Sanderson is a prolific fantasy author known for his intricate world-building and compelling characters. He has written numerous best-selling novels, including the "Mistborn" series, "The Stormlight Archive," and "The Wheel of Time" series (completing it after the original author, Robert Jordan, passed away). Sanderson's works are celebrated for their unique magic systems and engaging storytelling.
